What is Ideal Mother & Father (IMF) Therapy?

IMF Therapy is an innovative approach rooted in Ideal Parent Figure (IPF) methodology, designed to help individuals heal attachment wounds and develop a secure attachment style. By visualizing and internalizing the qualities of ideal parents, participants can rewire their neural pathways, leading to improved relationships, emotional regulation, and overall well-being.

Our program combines the core principles of IPF with additional elements from divine mother and father work, parts integration, and polarity techniques, offering a comprehensive approach to personal growth and healing.
